Group Reflection

Sanchit

I believe our group performed very well in this assignment, we were able to get a team plan ready very early on and executed it well. I found that I was able to complete the tasks assigned to me efficiently because everyone else was getting their sections done at a similar pace. If I had to improve upon anything it would probably be communication between the group, even though we got everything done there were certain member who were missing from many of our conversations/meetings. Something that was surprising was how we were able to use a lot of the knowledge we got from assignment 1 and 2 and apply it directly to sections in assignment 3. One thing I learnt about working in groups is being able to collaborate across different working hours, not everyone is available all the time so coordinating group work was a challenge.

Clinton

From this assignment, I found that our group was ahead of their games and each member contributed very well, as well as getting everything done on time if not before the deadline. In terms of what can be improved on, I would say, if possible, to have a proper meeting agenda and then to follow it during the meeting. This is because in our meeting, we just kind of wing it and go through topics randomly, making it hard to track. What was surprising was the difficulty in making the project idea into something that is somewhat functional, as I underestimated how difficult it was to use Bootstrap Studio. What I learnt about groups was that even if the workload seems large, with good teammates such as the ones in our group, the work feels easy with everyone's collaboration and contributions.

Vivek

In this assignment, I think everyone did their allocated work before on time and everyone did a fantastic job. As we are doing a group project, so we did an online MS Teams meeting to allocate work and we planned to do this assignment as early as possible. I discovered that I was able to accomplish my assigned task ahead of schedule. As a group, we performed very well. I believe we could have communicated more effectively, which would have aided us in completing this work considerably ahead of schedule. One thing I've learned about working in a group is to always trust your teammates. In a group project, everyone should believe that the other member will do their task on time and to the best of their abilities.

Navdeep

Personally, I believe what went well in this assignment, most definitely, would be that this time around we were ‘extremely’ organised, in regard to figuring out who does what(allocations). Additionally, at the commencement of this assignment we said “guys let’s try to finish this assignment as early as we possibly can”, and each and every one of us stuck by that. Everyone in the group was driven and motivated to finish this assignment early, and I would say we actually finished this assignment well before the due date. I don’t think there is much to be said when it comes to making a stamen on what could be improved. Only one thing I would say which could be improved would be that communication in this assignment, like the previous one, was lacking a little bit. I think we could have communicated more, which would have assisted us in finishing us this assignment well before the due date. One thing which was surprising was that this time around everyone in group clearly understood what they had to do, and they did not need explanation of anything. One thing I have learned about group is that organisation is key when it comes to working in a group, being organised is essential as it is an extremely important component of completing everything to the best of our abilities.
